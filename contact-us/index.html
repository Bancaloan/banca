Frequently asked questions

Can I pay off my loan early?

Yes, you can pay off your loan early. At Banca, we offer the flexibility to make early repayments on your loan. Paying off your loan ahead of schedule can have several benefits, including reducing the total interest you pay over the life of the loan and potentially improving your credit score.

If you're considering paying off your loan early or have questions about the process, please contact our customer support team. They'll be happy to assist you and provide you with all the necessary information to help you make an informed decision about your loan repayment.

How much can I Borrow From Banca?

The amount you can borrow from Banca depends on several factors, including your financial situation, credit history, the type of loan you're applying for, and the specific terms and conditions of the loan. Here's a general overview of what influences your borrowing limit:

Creditworthiness
Income and Debt-to-Income Ratio
Loan Type
Loan Purpose
Collateral
To find out how much you can borrow from Banca and to get a personalized loan offer, it's best to start by completing the online loan application or contacting our customer support team. We will assess your unique financial situation and provide you with loan options that align with your needs and eligibility.

Do you offering refinancing ?

Yes, we offer refinancing options at Banca. Refinancing is a valuable financial tool that allows you to replace an existing loan with a new one that may have more favorable terms, such as a lower interest rate, extended repayment period, or different loan structure.
To explore refinancing options with Banca, please contact our customer support team or visit our website for more information. We'll be happy to assist you in understanding the refinancing process and determining whether it's a suitable option for your financial needs. Keep in mind that eligibility criteria and terms may apply, so we recommend reaching out to our team for personalized guidance.

Can I do all of my banking with you?

No, Banca primarily specializes in providing loan and financial services. While we can assist you with various loan products, including personal loans, installment loans, and more, we do not offer full banking services such as checking and savings accounts.
For traditional banking services, including checking and savings accounts, you would need to contact a bank or financial institution directly. If you have any questions about the specific financial services we offer, please feel free to reach out to our customer support team, and we'll be happy to provide further assistance.

When should i apply?

The timing of your loan application depends on your individual financial needs and goals. At Banca, we accept loan applications year-round, allowing you to apply at your convenience.
